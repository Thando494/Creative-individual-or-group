Visual artist

loyiso Mkize born in Cape Town and grew up at Butterworth.

He discovered he can draw when he was Eight years old and at cretche he was complimented that he was an artist.His parents were supportive by exposing him at art fairs and bought art materials.

He started drawing seriously since grade one at eGcuwa Primary.He used to sell Bucs bunny cartoon character drawings for Five rand.He attended high school at Queens college boys in 2002 untill 2005.

loyiso Mkize studied Graphic Design at CPUT(Cape Peningsula University of Technology). He was a head illustrator at Strika Entertainment in 2008 at Woodstock,Cape Town.Loyiso enjoys enjoys telling stories by using visual art and comics.
